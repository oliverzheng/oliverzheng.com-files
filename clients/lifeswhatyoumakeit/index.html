How do you get your dreams out of your head, and into the world?

We are all artists of our own lives. Whether you are an accountant, an actor, a parent, a businessperson or a waitress; in every moment, you are the creator of your life. Whether you have dreams for your career, your health, your finances or your relationships, the power to bring those imaginings into reality lay in the hands of your ‘Inner Artist and the ability to ‘Make a Masterpiece of your Life’, is entirely up to you.

Lead by award winning, Internationally acclaimed artist and ‘spiritual intuitive’, Simone Wright; the day will be an inspiring, light hearted and truthful look at the patterns and often ‘invisible’ barriers we create that block our path to achieving the amazing, abundant and joyful life we dream of.

Through a series of stories, examples and exercises, Simone will teach you ...

  • How to identify the ‘invisible’ barriers to your progress
  • How to recognize the patterns of thought, behavior and Energy that you aren’t even aware of – that are keeping you from your highest potential.
  • The tools to ‘tune in’ to the frequencies of limitless creation and abundance
  • To recognize and instantly break old habits that are keeping you blocked
  • To feel the confidence and awareness that you have the power within to bring all of your greatest dreams into reality.

Your ability to create the life you have always imagined is within you. You alone are the artist of your life and nothing that you can imagine is unattainable. You only need to discover the tools.

Join Simone for this day of discovery and begin your own Renaissance.
