The A-Team
NBC, 1983-1987 - 5 Seasons
Starring: George Peppard, Eddie Valez, Mr. T, Dwight Schultz,
Dirk Benedict, Melinda Culea, Robert Vaughn

Antonio Saboto Jr. stars as a detective investigating a high school student in a class charged with a summer project to make a digital film. When one of the students produces a horror pic, and is roundly reprimanded by his teacher, the body count starts to add up.

The Greatest American Hero
ABC, 1980-1983 - 3 Seasons
Starring: William Katt, Connie Selleca, Robert Culp

An earnest high school teacher is presented with an unusual red flying suit from extraterrestrials and in spite of the fact that he promptly manages to lose the suit's operations manual, he's pressed into service as a crime fighter by an overly enthusiastic FBI agent with sometimes embarrassing results.
