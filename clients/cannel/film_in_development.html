Cannell is currently producing full length features in the thriller/horror genre in conjunction with IDT Entertainment. Click on the links below to view stills and read a story synopsis.

It Waits
Directed by Steve Monroe
Starring Corbin Bernsen, Antonio Sabato Jr., and Cindy Margolis

After her best friend is killed in a car accident in which she was driving, Forest Ranger Danielle St. Claire, moves into a secluded watchtower in the San Bernardino mountains to bury herself in her work. What she does not realize is, that a group of Princeton archeology students studying Native American culture, have opened a cave in the mountain and unwittingly released a murderous demon that was entombed there millions of years ago.

Demon Hunter
Directed by Scott Ziehl

When a priest in unable to exorcise the demon of a woman who had been possessed, JAKE must take matters into his own hands. He battles the demon and kills her. Jake is a demon hunter, working for the Catholic Church. He is trying to track down whoever, or whatever, it is that is responsible for the possession of women like the one he just killed.
