Questions from Stephen's novel "Vertical Coffin"
1. Who was Susan Smiley?
ANSWER: The twin of Paul Smiley, who was really Vincent Smiley dressed as a girl. (pg. 246)
2. What was the name of the Aerial Gunnery Range in the desert?
ANSWER: Chocolate Mountain Aerial Gunnery Range (pg. 296)
3. What was the motto of the Iron Pigs?
ANSWER: "Cut One and We All Bleed" (pg. 51)
4. What was the name of the Malibu Canyon biker hangout?
ANSWER: The Rock Store (pg. 3)
